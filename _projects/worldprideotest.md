<p>Protest poster for the World Prideotest Exhibition <a href="https://nolimitsartcastle.nl/evenement/world-pri%cc%b6d%cc%b6e%cc%b6otest/">the World Prideotest Exhibition</a> (2026) at No Limits! Art Castle in Amsterdam.</p>

<p>"Because now there is more to protest against than ever before. And every struggle is interconnected."</p>

<p>For my poster, I wanted to focus on contemporary extraction in Kazakhstan, and to address this call towards the governments of both Kazakhstan and the EU, the biggest investor into Kazakh critical mineral economy. Personal histories of family health issues due to pollution and labor and land extraction played a huge part in me choosing this specific concern.</p>
