<p>A poem exhibited on the storefront of Page Not Found, as part of their open call 'Open Letters'.</p>

<p>Page Not Found is a non-profit space based in The Hague, whose aim is to promote publishing as artistic practice, and to make artists’ publications and related writings accessible to a wider audience.</p>

<p>The poem was accompanied by a small fish sculpture. In Het Oude Centrum, T. Nachtegaal writes:</p>
<p>"The latest one, a poem written by Haider
Mukhit titled 'Caspia Cassiopeia' resonates with me perso-
nally. Eloquently, it starts with 'I exist between two ex-
tremes': Mukhit's words took me on several journeys and
allowed me to stop and reflect those aspects that touched
me, personally. I felt extremely lucky".</p>
