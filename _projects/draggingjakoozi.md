<p>Poster design for Dragging Liquid Ga(y)zes workshop and open call <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/DZXPTaxjAES/">as part of the World Prideotest Exhibition</a> at Jakoozi in Amsterdam.</p>

<p>“How can we use our bodies as resistance, pleasure, and protection in a world of state control and hyper-surveillance?" Queer & trans performers, s3x workers, and cultural workers, come together to explore embodied protest, protection, and new forms of visibility, through somatic performance, drag, improvisation, and camera work. This includes: Gazing through the Anal Eye, Anti-Surveillance Make Up Practice, Post-P0rn Camera Strategies & Aesthetics, and Impro Drag.</p>
