<p>First attempt at a modular type for an online class taken at the School of Poetic Computation, NYC.
Taught by Levi Hammett + Hind Al Saad. At the end of the class each of the student's works was compiled into
a zine (scans of which you can see here).</p>

<p>The spread with my modular typeface can be found outside this post in the general website page with my works.
The title of the typeface is Seyfulmaliq, inspired by Kazakh Persianate poetry.</p>
