<p>On Saturday 5 March, Kunstfort Vijfhuizen presented a live public programme on ecology, history, (human) bodies, dreams and languages. With work by artists from Central and North Asia. The works make layered histories visible: traces of the colonial and Soviet past and the contemporary reality of capitalist exploitation.</p>

<p>With works by Saodat Ismailova, Haider Timur, Natalia Papaeva, co-curated by and with a text by researcher Fabienne Rachmadiev. Moderated by Zippora Elders.</p>
